Running Distances

  • 1km Run

    This short and sweet 1km run is perfect for beginners or those who just want a bit of fun. Whether you're looking to kickstart your fitness journey or simply enjoy a leisurely jog, it's a great way to begin.

  • 5km Run

    With a distance of 5km, this run is a solid challenge for beginner runners or those seeking to improve their fitness. Whether you're looking to increase speed or endurance, this distance will test your limits.

  • 10km Run

    The 10km run is an excellent intermediate distance requiring a good balance of speed and stamina. A bit more dedication in training brings a worthy sense of accomplishment at the finish line.

  • Half Marathon

    At 21km, the half marathon demands consistent training and resilient mindset. This milestone combines both physical and mental endurance for a truly rewarding achievement.

  • Full Marathon

    The ultimate 42km test of fortitude, the full marathon requires months of rigorous preparation. Conquering this event delivers a life-changing sense of triumph.
